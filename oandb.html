Creative Director, Orange and Blue Magazine Summer 2014

Every semester the students enrolled in UF's JOU4445c course work together to create a magazine for that semester. They create a theme and design concept and work together to produce stories and photos to accompany them. I was one of the creative directors for the Summer 2014 issue, and our theme was energy. I worked with the other creative director as well as two art directors to choose the fonts, color palette and design aspects.


The fonts used were Oswald, Roboto. Roboto Slab and Roboto Condensed. Sparing you the CMYK specifics, our color palette consisted of a grey, light green, green, blue and indigo.
