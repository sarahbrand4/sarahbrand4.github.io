Editor-in-Chief and Sports and Clubs Editor, Bloomingdale Senior High School Minotaur

     I was part of Minotaur's yearbook staff from my sophomore year through my senior year (2007-2010). I began at the bottom of the chain working as a staff writer and photographer. Junior year I was Sports and Clubs Editor, and I designed every sports and clubs feature and group photos, as well as other weekly double trucks.By senior year I was editor-in-chief. I designed the opening and closing spreads and oversaw the editors, design stall and overall production of the yearbook.

     Our 2010 edition, Add Me, was an NSPA Design of the Year Finalist, CSPA Gold Medalist, SIPA Excellent and FSPA All-Floridian. Our 2009 edition, Life, Captured Daily, won six Gold Circle Awards for the first time in Minotuar history.

     I personally won First Place for the Carry-In Yearbook Sports Spread and Third Place for the On-the-Spot Yearbook Team at the 2009 FSPA District Contest. At the 2008 FSPA District Contest I won Second Place in the Yearbook Writing, Editing and Design Team contest.
