Redesign, Seventeen Magazine

This was a redesign of Seventeen Magazine I did as an assignment in a design elective at UF. I chose to redesign Seventeen initially because I think teenage girls should be shown there is more to life than boys, makeup and expensive clothes and jewelery. I also think overtime Seventeen's spreads have become cluttered and immature. In my redesign I strived to showcase stories in a more mature, but colorful, clean way.


The fonts used were Helevetica Neue and Minion Pro. Sparing you the CMYK specifics, my color palette consisted of blue, purple, yellow, green, grey and pink.
